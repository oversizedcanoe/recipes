## Tartar Sauce
Last time I made this, I made 1/4 of it and it was still too much for two servings.

### The Gist
No gist.

---

### Detailed Recipe
#### Ingredients
 - 3tbsp pickles
 - 1tbsp capers
 - 1tbsp parsley
 - 1/2 a small white onion, or maybe better a shallot
 - 1c mayo
 - 1 clove garlic
 - 1/2 tbsp lemon juice 

#### Steps
1. Finely dice the pickles, capers, parley, onion, and garlic.
2. Mix everything together.
3. Taste and adjust accordingly. If necessary, add salt, sugar, or more lemon juice. If it's too strong, add more mayo.
