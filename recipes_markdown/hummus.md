### Detailed Recipe
#### Ingredients
 - 1 can drained and rinsed chickpeas
 - 1 clove garlic
   - I love garlic but since it's raw, 1 clove is MORE than enough
 - 2-4tbsp tahini
 - Lemon juice, maybe 2tsp to start but may add more
- Big pinch of salt
- Olive oil, maybe 1tbsp
  - Could try sesame too 

#### Steps
1. Blend all the ingredients in a blender or ideally with a stickblender.
2. Add water as needed to reach desired texture.
3. Taste and adjust tahini, lemon, and salt.
